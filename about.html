About the Project

“Cacao de Origen” is a FONTAGRO-funded initiative led by Universidad Nacional de Costa Rica (UNA), focused on strengthening the cacao value chain in Central America through training, open results, and dialogue.

  • Leads: Project team at UNA
  • Scope: Webinars, sampling, and technical studies (sensory, chemistry, microbiology)
  • Region: Central America
